# AI Artisan Empowerment Platform

## Problem
Many skilled workers like tailors and beauticians cannot grow their business due to lack of digital knowledge and awareness.

## Solution
Our AI platform helps them:
- Analyze their work
- Suggest design improvements
- Discover government schemes
- Learn branding and business growth

## Features
- AI Work Analyzer
- Learning Path Suggestions
- Branding Guidance
- Government Scheme Support
